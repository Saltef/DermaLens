# Medical and Privacy Disclaimer

DermaLens is a portfolio and research prototype. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, prevent, or monitor disease.

The app returns screening-style visual observations from a local image classifier. Results can be wrong, incomplete, or biased by lighting, camera quality, skin tone representation, image framing, and the limited public datasets used during development.

Do not use this project for medical decisions. Concerning, changing, painful, bleeding, persistent, or rapidly spreading skin findings should be reviewed by a qualified clinician.

By default, the app is designed to run locally, strip uploaded image metadata by re-encoding images, and avoid saving uploads unless explicitly configured. Users are still responsible for securing their own machine, browser, Docker environment, and any datasets or private photos used with the project.
